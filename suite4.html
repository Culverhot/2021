SUITE 4

One Bedroom Budget Suite, Ground Floor

Previous Next × Close

Charming 1 bedroom compact suite tucked away behind the main building  with own separate entrance accessed via 2 shallow steps with handrail. Comprising one bed/living room with double bed and two seater leather sofa with en-suite shower bathroom and adjoining Kitchen dining room. We term it a budget suite because it is smaller than our other Suites and doesn't have any views looking out at woodland on one side and our wood cabin laundry to the side. This is reflected in the pricing.
 However if you are out in the view all day this may not be key to you and it is a comfortable, well equipped and economic place to stay. 

What's in the Suite  ?

Double (140cm) bed
All bedding, towels and toileteries
Kitchen with full oven and hob
Refridgerator with small freezer compartment
Kettle, filter coffee machine
Dishwasher
Cleaning materials - dishwasher tablets, washing up liquid, tea towels
Pots, pans and a full array of utensils
Crockery and cutlery
There is a pack of fresh filter coffee, tea bags milk and sugar
50in TV with Sky movies and catch up TV
Free WiFi
Alexa dot speaker
