Windsurfing & Surfing

 

Port Eynon is a great bay for windsurfing and as we are so close to the beach it is ideal - not too far to carry your gear!

There are a number of surf schools on Gower, Caswell, Langland and Rhosilli are the best surfing beaches. Click on the photo and the more button to link to surf schools.

Walking

There are many lovely walks to take accross and around the Gower peninsular. We have walking guides in each of the suites - circular walks on Gower are particularly popular as you end up back where you started out. The bus system on Gower is a little challenging but connections can be found to get you back from the end of a walk with some thought and planning.
